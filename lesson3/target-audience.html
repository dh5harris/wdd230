Our Target Audience

The Chamber's target audience is the businesses in the region and the people that live there. The Chamber wants to be able to help businesses connect with other resources, like other businesses, citizens, commmuntiy partners, and elected officials. Doing this will make the commmuntiy economy stronger.

Business Owners

Business Owners need help to find the people and other businesses resources to help them make connections and grow.

  • Scenario 1 - A new business owner that is trying to find someone to help get the word out.
  • Scenario 2 - The long time business owner has need of new or updated services. An example, finding someone to update their website because the neighbor kid, "John", who made the website ten years ago while still in high school has moved
  • Scenario 3 - A way for business owners to know how new laws might effect their business.

Customers

Everyone is a customer, even businesses

  • Scenario 1 - A family moved into the area. They are needing to find a plumber in the area to fix their leak.
  • Scenario 2 - Someone that is looking to find what resturants that are in the area.
