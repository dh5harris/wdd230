The St. Louis Temple

The St. Louis Missouri Temple located in the St. Louis suburb of Town and Country near the junction of I-64 and I-270. The St. Louis temple was the first temple built in Missouri even though three temple sites have been dedicated in the 1830's. It is the 50th temple that is dedicated and still in use, minus the temples under renovations.
